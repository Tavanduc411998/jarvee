Unknown Strategies For Growing Youtube Subscribers

Today is a very competitive world, where every one of us is looking at how we can improve our business. Be it the IT world, the restaurant field or just anything, we want customers to be attracted to us as honey bees get attracted to the honey.

So true! Just another option of making business is the YouTube World where people are competing with each other in getting the maximum number of subscribers for their YouTube Channel.

And to win this kind of attention, we need to have lots of subscribers. So why not discuss how one can easily and smartly work on getting the maximum number of subscribers for the YouTube Channel, and win the hearts of people around the globe quite very easily.

For this, no you do not need any tricks or hacks but just hold on to the tactics that will surely work!

We have always seen that since the past few years, social media channels like Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, etc., have been able to steal the social limelight quite easily. But one must also agree that YouTube is still an incredible marketing channel where it has been getting more than 32 million visitors per day and more than 5 billion video views each day.

Therefore, if you have just started on to build your YouTube channel and are finding out ways that can help you to optimize the existing content, then go ahead and read this.

1.  Get Smarter, stay original

Remember to stop wasting your time looking for YouTube shortcuts. All you need to do is build up a proper plan to create original videos. Creating quality content is more important than anything else. Content that is substantial and speaks to you as well as your audience will click without the need for any gimmick. So, be sure to produce content that you are passionate about and would like your audience to know of.

When you are done, start promoting your YouTube channel. For this, you will have to begin with manual tasks. And once you have some traction, you will see that the organic traffic will start to appear.

2.  Optimization is the key

Next, for attracting more subscribers to your YouTube channel, you need to be visible on YouTube. For this, think of optimizing your videos and channel by making use of relevant techniques in your videos.

  • Be thematic
    You can do some background study before selecting a topic on which your videos are going to be made. It should depend on the demographic as well.
  • Add popular keywords
    YouTube’s algorithm will better suggest those videos having popular keywords as viewing suggestions.
  • Polish the description
    Build a credible channel description by keeping in mind your brand and style. This will help you in connecting to the right audience. By focussing on your channel description you will also attract new subscribers who are interested in your style of content.

    Consider employing keyword planner techniques and other tools of research before deciding on your video’s title and description.

  • Add better thumbnails
    Often blamed to be the reason for click-baiting, a better-designed thumbnail image can work like magic to increase the views and in turn, the subscriber count.
  • Offer transcript
    The viewer, as well as YouTube, better regard a video having a transcript. This will have a positive effect on your subscriber count.
  • Stay in your niche
    This point has to do with the content quality. Choosing a niche and sticking to it can work wonders on your YouTube channel. It will also gain the trust of your subscribers when you do not fluctuate between irrelevant topics.
  • Follow viral trends
    It is good to promote a viral trend and do a short vide on them every once in a while.  Saves you the trouble from being called a caveman.
  • Collab
    Collaboration is a good way to increase your views as well as Subscriber count. It has the added advantage that your content will attract both sides’ subscriber base.
  • Inter-link videos
    Making good use of your End Screen is also an effective way to bump up your subscribers number. You can link your other videos in the end or a complete playlist to lead your viewers to your content, and if you are able to keep your content strong, you can earn as many subscribers as you want. This will also likely save you from spending a whole lot of money on ads.

3.  Use quality software to edit

Editing both your videos is another key to attracting more subscribers. While a cool and clean channel will tell your viewers what it is all about, the right quality editing will make sure they convert to your subscribers. Maintaining a crisp look and feel will suggest that you are a professional and earn you extra points for credibility.

4.  Engage with followers

Engage with your followers as much as possible. This will ensure that you build a particularly strong connection with your existing subscriber base and attract new ones. Replying to comments shows your credibility and also your ability to involve with strangers, attracting new subscribers.

5.  Upload frequently

Maintaining a strict rhythm with your video uploads will make you more popular among your viewers. Notably, one video in a week has been found to help in maintaining a steady number of subscribers. But depending on your content, you can release your videos to suit your style.

6.  Amazon marketing strategy

Another popular method is that you find popular books or products related to your YouTube channel and try to get a genuine review on Amazon where you also mention your YouTube channel in the end which will help people to search for it in Google.

7.  Facebook promotions

Another way of marketing is the most common way which all utilizes which is the Facebook Groups where you add comments telling people about a YouTube video that you enjoyed. For this kind of work, you can also outsource a freelancer.

Let’s sum it up!

With numerous videos being uploaded every day on YouTube, it certainly becomes difficult to stand out in the crowd. Using these strategies is sure to give an upper hand and hence, make your channel popular.

Have a try and let us know.
