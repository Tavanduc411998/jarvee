How to Become Instagram famous: 10 expert tips from real-time influencer

Nowadays, being an Instagram famous has become essential for surviving in the social media economy. The terms and conditions for each social media platform differ, so it is obvious Instagram-famous is not similar to Facebook one. On Instagram, the popularity of a person is measured by the number of followers.

It is your reach among the masses that paints you as a successful influencer on Instagram. Frankly, there is no set of a formula for becoming Instagram famous as such. It demands you to strategize, educate, and understand the world that you are entering into at-great length.

If you are just starting on this journey, you might have certain role models, whom you would like to follow. Well, a little help and guidance do no harm in this matter. So, we have tried to extend a helping hand through this article by sharing some of the expert tips of the forum.

Tips from Real-time Influencers on Instagram

These tips come from the best in the business, who made a place for themselves in the crowd of many.

1. Don’t Be a Burnout 

Do not be driven by the noise of the crowd, rather follow the music of the soul, search for the passion. Most of the influencers inevitably rank finding the passion at the top. It is the passion that will help you to push just that extra to make things happen.

Generally, it is observed that people are badly influenced by the vanity of someone else’s fame and steer away from doing the things that they love to do.

2. Persist with the Passion

Initially, if you get swayed by the crowd, the delusion kicks in for about a month or so, and individuals start with vigor, but as time elapses the intent goes down- the burn out begins.

On the other hand, if you choose an area or field which you are passionate about, you persist- you keep creating content.

When you are driven by passion, it hardly matters what the numbers are. You just look at the bigger picture of being unique and creating a space for yourself. Finally, with lots of persistence, you get a loyal fan base, who stick with you at all time for your content.

3. Keep Yourself and Your Feed Updated Regularly

In this dynamic world, learning is a continuous process. You just can’t learn all the things in life. The changing nature of the medium technically and otherwise forces you to keep up with it. If you keep pace with change, you will eventually become redundant.

On the technical side, it is important to note that the algorithm of the medium keeps changing. Also, you don’t need to be a computer wizard to track down changes. Just with some help and observation, the changes or at least the implication of the same can be ascertained.

Trends do change with time, this is where you as an influencer must be proactive enough. The best way to keep up with the changing trend is to post regularly. An updated feed and the response will have you understood the nature of the content that sticks with the audience for that given period of time.

4. Feed on the Content that’s On the Other Side 

To be –hashtag– Instagram famous, you need to be out there and make yourself visible. The process requires new content to be churned out periodically, but it is not easier said than done.

It is not always possible to come with great ideas for the next video post or just an instant story. This is the time where you draw inspiration from the fragmented pieces of ideas available in the comment section or just feedback. Sometimes, criticism also forms a crucial source of content ideas.

You may not find the idea of lying there in the comment section. But you have to read in between the lines and engage with the followers intensively.

5. Nature of the Content

Just posting for the sake of it won’t earn you brownie points. The random hashtags also won’t add to any positive results. You need to understand there are many others like you trying to make it big. Thus, it becomes to get to act together and publish content as relevant and useful as it can be.

The posts must have a narrative attached to them, they must communicate a story effectively. There’s no point going to teach something, you don’t need to provide validation for your knowledge base on the subject.

If you have followers coming back to your page, it is understood that your expertise has been validated by the audience itself. It is up to you to find new ways to keep them engaged and informed at the same time.

6. Do not Go for an Instant Fan Base

You may not prefer to be an example of 15-minute fame. Trying to create an instant fanbase is futile in a larger scheme of things. Also, the resources went into gaining the likes and follows are futile. Brands today have their work cut out when it comes to drawing a line between acquired and paid fame.

They have a team dedicated to finding the authenticity of the information that is presented on your bio. So, it is suggested by the various famous influencers to not to worry about brand integration but to focus on user engagement. Today, the most common way to achieve quality user engagement is through contests and giveaways.

7. Being Yourself 

Being yourself here essentially means being fearless about being vulnerable. The first and foremost thing as a social media influencer is that you must let go of your inhibitions. There will be times when you would like to hold back expecting a fear-based reaction.

This is the time when you need to tell yourself that this is my followers- my social media family, and there is nothing wrong in telling the truth or admitting faults. This is the best part of social people’s perception changes within weeks. So, you need to embrace originality and just be yourself.

8. Looking Out for a Collaboration

You may be great at your craft, but reaching a new audience is not just about being good. Increasing the user base in a new location with different ethnicity requires a native to send the message. This is where you would need the collaboration with a regional influencer who probably speaks the same tongue, and nullify the language barrier.

Source

9. Keeping Track of Numbers 

Being a famous Instagram influencer goes beyond creating thought-provoking content. You must also have a knack of looking into numbers and formulating strategies.

For starters, you must have an Instagram business page that provides all the relevant data points like- count of impressions, link clicks, profile visits and so on. Analyzing, these data points can come in real handy in both the short and long run.

10. Always Remember the Reason

Last but not the least, you need to be true to the reason why you started it all. If quick money is the driver of your ambition it won’t last too long.

In such a situation even the smallest obstacle will knock you out. But, if you know the reason and it’s not instant money, then you will invariably put in hard work and fame will be the byproduct.

A Snapshot of How to Be Instagram Famous

As mentioned earlier, there are no rules on Instagram to possess a quick Insta-fame. The key is basically to know your passion and work around it, which is absolutely the human aspect.

On the other hand, the no-human side of it is the tech and numbers. A perfect mix of both will eventually lead you to be the next biggest Instagram Influencer.
