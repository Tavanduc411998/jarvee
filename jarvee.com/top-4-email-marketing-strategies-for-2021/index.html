Top 4 Email Marketing Strategies for 2021

Source: Freepik

Despite the rise of social media and the fact that some traditional marketing channels and tools have become somewhat undervalued, they still play a huge role in the overall success of your campaigns. And one of such tools that are still effective is email.

Email Marketing: Why Still Care About It in 2021?

After years of being around, email marketing is still on the rise and delivers results. Both stats and real cases confirm this.

Here are a few facts to prove the effectiveness of this tool.

  • Now, over 290 billion emails are sent every day, and this number is expected to grow to 347 billion by the end of 2023.
  • Compared to other tools and channels, email is proven to have a higher ROI rating (73%).
  • It’s one of the most cost-effective and yet profitable channels for marketing. For every $1 spent, you can get on average $51.

Based on these facts, we can confidently say that email marketing still matters a lot these days. However, the fact that it still around does not mean that the strategies haven’t changed.

Read on to learn what effective strategies are there for this year.

Source: Freepik

4 Strategies to Make the Most of Your Email Marketing Efforts in 2021

Looking for effective ways to use email for marketing purposes? Here, we compiled the top four strategies that bring the best results in 2021.

1.    Shift Towards Mobile

There’s no doubt we are living in the era of mobile devices. Today, people prefer performing most tasks using convenient, small devices.

According to stats, the number of marketing messages opened on a mobile device is growing rapidly. In 2011, this number was 27%. In 2016, it jumped up to 55%. But, today, this number has crossed the point of 81%, which is impressive!

But what does it mean in terms of email marketing? In a nutshell, these numbers suggest that if you don’t keep mobile-friendliness in mind, you are not getting the most of your campaigns.

Messages that are optimized for mobile are proven to be more effective and generate 4x higher revenues. So, the first marketing strategy you need to stick to in 2021 is mobile optimization.

Here are a few actionable tips that will help you make this shift.

  • Switch to responsive email design (RED) that ensures smooth optimization of the user experience (UX) to all screens and devices.
  • Make your pre-header and subject line shorter so that users of mobile devices could see them fully.
  • Make sure your CTAs are big and easy to notice.
  • Use email to SMS solution

2.    Deliver Personalized Messages

The next strategy you should employ in your campaigns is personalization. Today, when an average user receives hundreds of messages every single day, getting your emails noticed (and, hopefully, opened) requires more effort than ever. That’s where personalization is the key to success!

Using customer data to personalize the messages you deliver is a sure way to boost overall customer experience, which eventually will lead to greater loyalty and retention. Besides, personalization has been proven to boost open rates. It can bring you 6x higher transaction rates, which is even more important.

There’s one more thing you need to know. Despite the undeniable benefits of personalization, many brands still don’t use this strategy. That is, when you begin personalizing your messages, it will ultimately put you ahead of the competition.

How to get started with personalization? First of all, there are two things you should do:

  • start using a recipient’s name in the subject line (as well as in the main text of the message);
  • start collecting customer data (for example, via a sign-up form) to get the information you need for better personalization.

3.    Benefit from Email Segmentation

Segmentation is one of the main trends of the past years that remains with us in 2021. So, this is one more strategy to keep in mind to maximize your outcomes.

In a nutshell, segmenting your database implies dividing your contacts list into smaller groups of customers or prospects who share certain traits and features. This trick enables you to create more personalized messages for every group based on its characteristics and deliver much more targeted messages. Eventually, segmentation will help you boost your brand’s reputation and improve all KPIs.

If you are wondering how to get started, consider segmenting your list by any of the following characteristics:

  • company size;
  • industry;
  • sales cycle.

Doing so will give you a good starting point, and later, you will be able to tweak your segmentation strategy further to get even better results.

4.    Leverage Automation to Maximize Efficiency

Since marketing automation has been trending for quite a while, we expect it to stay popular for years.

Today, 75% of marketers are already using automation to their benefit. There are multiple ways to integrate automation into your tactic. It can be used to streamline your campaigns, collect feedback, set up funnels, create trigger emails, and so on.

Automation has numerous perks. It not only saves lots of time for your team, but it can also improve the open and click-through rates and boost conversion. The great part of it is that automation is now available to everyone. There are plenty of smart tools that will help you get on the right track and achieve the set goals. All you need is to test these tools and find what works best for you!

The Bottom Line

To get the best results from your campaigns in 2021, you need to update your approach to email marketing to ensure it goes in line with the time. Luckily, after checking out this guide, you are well aware of the four most effective strategies to look out for this year. They include

  • mobile optimization
  • personalization
  • segmentation
  • automation

Are you already using any of these? If not, it might be just the right time to tweak your overall strategy. If yes, let us know which strategies you found the most successful!
