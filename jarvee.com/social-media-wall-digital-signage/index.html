How Displaying Social Media Wall on Digital Signage Helps Restaurant Owners To Boost Revenue

The constant shift in technology gave rise to many digital devices, including digital signage. Digital signage is a digital screen with a strong presence that cannot be ignored or overlooked.

Digital signages have helped sectors in a variety of ways to widen out their reach and attract their potential customers. The restaurant industry has also adopted various means and strategies to maximize its reach, enhance customer engagement and sales of all the industries and sectors.

The restaurant’s digital signage has made its way to becoming an increasingly popular tool in many ways. However, it is essential to know and get clarity on digital signage and the content to be displayed for maximum benefits.

In this post, we will tell you about the different ways you can leverage restaurant digital signage in your business and make the most of it with the correct content. Without any further ado, let’s get started!

Best Content Ideas To Display On A Restaurant Digital Signage

●      Include A Social Media Wall

To gain more attention and engagement from your customers, you can include a social media wall on your restaurant’s digital signage. Social media is a vast pool of content that can be aggregated and displayed on digital signage to gain maximum attention from your customers.

You can aggregate the feeds from various social media channels using a social media aggregation tool, customize it according to your likings and display it in the form of a social media wall. Additionally, you can even showcase the content uploaded by your customers on their social media handles using your restaurant’s hashtag.

The customers in a restaurant post content in various forms on their social media handles. It can be in selfies, pictures, videos, etc.

Such content can be referred to as User-generated Content. Placing it on digital signage can help brands build social proof and push customers to try out their recommendations.

●      Display Reviews & Testimonials Of Customers

Like other industries and sectors, the restaurant industry also needs to display the validation of its customers to get more sales & conversions. People highly use the internet as a medium to express their views about brands and their experiences with products. User-Generated Content is the new-age content marketing strategy for brands to maximize their profits and build brand authenticity among the customers in today’s marketing space.

User-Generated content refers to the content that is created by the existing users of a brand, and it can be in the form of photos, videos, GIFs, etc. The reason why it has emerged to become a favorite trend for marketers is since it is the most authentic, trustworthy, and genuine form of content. It builds trust among the customers and attracts them more towards the brand.

Showcasing the reviews left by your customers, including their recommendations on digital signage, is a great move to gain the trust of your potential customers and make them order the most recommended items.

●      Digital Menu Boards

Those days are long gone when restaurants used paper menus for serving their customers. Instead, restaurants have gone digital now. Digital menu boards have been around for quite some time now and have prevailed to become one of the best inventions for restaurants.

Restaurants can use digital signage to display their menu and the nutritional information/value of the food items. They can give details into their bestselling meals, prices of the food items, combo offers, and much more. Moreover, it is an excellent way of saving on paper and thinking about preserving the environment.

Additionally, it helps customers quicken the process of placing the order without going through the tedious process of surfing through the paper menus. Also, attractive food images and offers displayed on the digital menu boards intrigue customers to purchase more.

●      Use It In Waiting Rooms

Another great way of using a restaurant’s digital signage is by putting it on digital signage and placing it in the waiting room. You can display a variety of content on the digital screen. It can be a collection of pictures of the ambience of the restaurant, food items, food prices, special offers, and much more. You can also display drool-worthy pictures of the bestsellers.

Doing so keeps the customers engaged, and they look forward to trying out the restaurant. Moreover, it reduces the perceived wait time of the customers.

●      Helps In Upselling

Another great way of using digital signage in restaurants is upselling and cross-selling food items. You can display a collection of drool-worthy pictures, attractive food images, or the newly launched food products followed by a few enticing captions.

You can mention captions like ‘Upgrade to a bigger meal by paying just 2$’ or ‘Free Peri-Peri Fries with a large meal’, etc. This would help drive more sales and conversions since it would intrigue the customers to purchase more instantly.

●      Display Exclusive Offers

Another fantastic way of using digital signage in the restaurant is to display special discounts and offers to your customers. Displaying discounts on a large screen is extremely tough to overlook. So display any ongoing deal or upcoming sale to attract more customers and enhance their engagement in your restaurant.

Moreover, if your customers like your meals and food, they would be compelled to purchase more at minimum prices. Doing so would work great to gain attention from your customers, upscale profits, and generate revenue.

Final Thoughts

This brings us to the end of this post, and you are now aware of the use-cases of digital signage in restaurants and how it can work tremendously to grow your business and enhance customer engagement with your brand.

Apart from its strong presence, digital signage also holds the potential to display relevant information, engage the audience, and much more!

Digital signage has time and again proven its incredible impact and how it can benefit businesses. If you have not yet used the tool for the growth of your business, then you are missing out on a lot.

Go ahead now, install digital signage in your restaurant business, and you will be amazed at how it can benefit your business in the ways mentioned above!
