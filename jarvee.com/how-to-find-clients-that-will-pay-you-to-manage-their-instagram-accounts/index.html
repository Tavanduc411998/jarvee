How to Find Clients That Will Pay You to Manage Their Instagram Accounts

One of the most common questions coming from those who want to work and earn money online is actually this, where to start and how to find clients. In this article, we will give some suggestions specifically for those who want to find their clients on Instagram.

Even there are so many users on Instagram and so many businesses that really need help with managing their profiles, getting clients is a challenging task. Here we will share with you some tips that will give you more chances to gain new clients.

Profile adjustments and trust factor

Before starting with any kind of search and reaching out to your potential clients, you need to polish your profile from which you will be contacting these potential users. Our recommendation is to do it from a real profile that belongs to you or your agency/company. Your profile is your first impression and if you expect someone to be happy to hire to for managing their Instagram profile, you should show them that you know how to do this.

It’s important that your profile has a trust factor. If you have some previous work, results, testimonials, or anything that it’s some kind of legit proof that you can deliver what you are promising, you should share it on your profile. This kind of content is welcome in any form, but it would be convenient to have this information saved in your IG highlights so that those that visit your profile can easily get to know more about you and what you do.

Pay attention to the contact details that you provided on the profile, make sure that they are transparent and correct and that interested users can reach you easily.
Another beneficial information on your profile is a link to your website or landing page where you can take advantage to say more about you and why they should hire you for this service. If you have resources, this addition will be beneficial for you. You can also learn how to create your own website without knowing how to code and save some money if this is an area of our interest.

Things to figure out before starting your search

Let’s face this first, even if you are the best in what you do, you can’t say that your target audience is everyone and that what you are doing is suitable for any niche. Since this is a fact, you should first think about your ideal potential client. We will assume that you have at least some experience with managing accounts and that you already worked for some clients. Generate a list of a few different niches where you feel comfortable working with and where you can show your full potential. Especially if part of your services is creating content for your clients.

Working online gave us more freedom when it comes to communications and collaboration with people from different parts of the world. That means that your clients can be anywhere, as long and you can communicate on the same language.

We will now give you an example of how you can search and extract your potential clients easily, in just a few steps with Jarvee. This method allows you to extract potential clients efficiently and with respect to the parameters that suit your needs. You can adjust your parameters, language, and sources as per your preferences, the example that we want to show you is just a general example for one of the most popular niches on IG.

Extracting Users using the Follow tool Settings

For the extraction of the potential client, you can use Jarvee Follow tool. On the settings page of this tool, you need to set basic settings and filters that will give you more targeted results for what you are looking for.

It’s important that you check the option to send to extracted users so that the list of extracted users is generated on the Extracted Users tab.

1. This source is awesome if you want to extract users that use hashtag fitness in their posts. This means that their content is related to this topic and they could be your potential clients.

You can also set in the Follow tool Settings section – filter User bio/username/name must contain any of the following words and use for example word trainer. A combination of this source and filter can give some deeper search where you will find trainers that use fitness as hashtags.

2. Second useful source for this situation is to Follow by full name/username/bio. Here you can search for the profiles that will have this specific word in their full name, username, or bio. A very simple and straightforward source that can give you plenty of valuable results.

3. This combination can be appropriate if you want to search for users that posted posts with specific locations, or check some of the two available additional options related to Geo location, if this is what is more suitable for your search.

An interesting way of using this source is that you can combine this source with some of the filters from the Follow tool settings where you can specify some words from your own or some other specific desired language and this way you can get more targeted results, especially if you want to target clients from some specific location.

There are plenty of other sources options (see below) that you can take advantage of, those mentioned above are what would be suitable in most cases.

 

Once you set the settings and sources and start your Follow tool, list of extracted users will be available on the Extracted Users tab. From there you can use this list further for contacting these potential clients or for other purposes.

Now you found the list of your potential clients and you are one step closer to reaching them. To do this search manually, you would need much more time, and Jarvee allows you to do this in just a few steps.
